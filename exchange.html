AIF Token Economics

The foundation of value exchange within the ecosystem

AIF Platform Token

The AIF token serves as the primary medium of exchange within the Hong Kong AI International Film Festival ecosystem, with a fixed total supply of 1 billion tokens. It is designed to create a sustainable economic model that aligns the interests of all ecosystem participants.

Key Utilities

Governance

AIF token holders have voting rights on platform development, fee structures, and policy decisions

Fee Discounts

Users paying fees with AIF tokens receive significant trading fee discounts

Priority Access

Holders gain privileged access to new film token launches and exclusive content releases

Value Capture

Regular token burns from exchange fees create deflationary mechanics that benefit long-term holders

Film-Specific Tokens

Each AI film project on the platform can issue its own unique token, representing sharing rights to future revenue and creative decision-making rights for that specific project.

FilmToken Standard

Key Characteristics

Revenue Sharing

Token holders receive a proportion of film's revenue across all distribution channels

Creative Input

Opportunity to participate in creative decisions through governance mechanisms

Secondary Market Liquidity

Tokens tradable on AIF Exchange from pre-production through distribution

Smart Contract Governance

Automated dividend distribution and transparent milestone tracking

Film tokens create a revolutionary model for film financing and audience engagement, allowing fans and investors to participate directly in a project's success while providing creators with new funding avenues and community-building tools.

Value Capture Mechanisms

Service Fee Value Capture

A portion of all transaction fees collected on the exchange is used to buy back and burn AIF tokens, creating continuous deflationary pressure that benefits all token holders.

Project Success Sharing

The exchange receives a small percentage of revenue from successful film projects launched on the platform, a portion of which is distributed to AIF stakers as rewards.

AI Service Revenue

Premium AI analytics and market prediction services generate additional revenue streams for the platform, with a portion allocated to token value enhancement.

Security & Compliance

Industry-leading safeguards for digital assets and regulatory compliance

Multi-Layer Security Architecture

Cold & Hot Wallet Separation

The majority of funds are stored in air-gapped cold wallets, with only a small percentage kept in hot wallets for immediate liquidity needs, significantly reducing the risk of large-scale security breaches.

Multi-Signature Authentication

Critical operations require multiple approvals through a distributed multi-signature system, eliminating single points of failure and protecting against internal threats.

Advanced Encryption Protocols

All data is protected with military-grade encryption, secure communication channels, and hardware security modules (HSMs) for key management.

Global Compliance Strategy

  • Comprehensive KYC/AML Procedures

    Rigorous identity verification and anti-money laundering systems that meet or exceed regulatory requirements in all operating jurisdictions

  • Regulatory Licensing

    Proactive acquisition of relevant licenses and registrations in key markets to ensure full legal compliance

  • Transaction Monitoring

    Advanced AI-powered systems to identify suspicious activities and ensure compliance with global anti-terrorism financing regulations

  • Regulatory Engagement

    Active collaboration with regulators to develop appropriate frameworks for the emerging tokenized film asset class

Additional Security Measures
