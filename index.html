重新定義電影藝術 Redefining Cinema Art

我們正站在電影藝術歷史上的一個關鍵轉折點。HKAIIFF不是要成為現有模式的"AI版本"， 而是要構建一個全新的、原生於AI時代的電影藝術評價與展示體系。

We are at a critical turning point in the history of cinematic art. HKAIIFF is not about becoming an "AI version" of existing models, but about building a brand new, native evaluation and display system for cinematic art in the AI era.

從"作者論"到"指揮家論"的範式轉移

Paradigm Shift from "Auteur Theory" to "Conductor Theory"

傳統"作者論"

Traditional "Auteur Theory"

  • • 導演作為"作者"，個人願景是創作核心
  • • Director as "author", personal vision as creative core
  • • 所有部門圍繞固定藍圖工作
  • • All departments work around a fixed blueprint
  • • 線性的創作流程
  • • Linear creative process
  • • 人工創造每個元素
  • • Manual creation of every element

AI時代"指揮家論"

AI Era "Conductor Theory"

  • • 創作者如交響樂指揮家
  • • Creator as symphony conductor
  • • 通過精準指令工程引導AI系統
  • • Guiding AI systems through precise prompt engineering
  • • 人機協同的創作過程
  • • Human-machine collaborative creative process
  • • "引導"、"選擇"和"整合"AI生成內容
  • • "Guiding", "selecting" and "integrating" AI-generated content
🎬

AI原生創作範式

AI-Native Creative Paradigm

從"作者論"到"指揮家論"的轉變，創作者通過精準的指令工程、模型調教和系統設計， 引導人類與AI組成的複雜系統共同完成藝術創作。

Transition from "auteur theory" to "conductor theory", where creators guide complex systems composed of humans and AI through precise prompt engineering, model fine-tuning, and system design to complete artistic creation.

四大革命性電影類別 Four Revolutionary Film Categories

基於AI技術參與度和交互模式，建立四大核心類別， 突破傳統電影節以時長和製作模式為主的分類局限

Establishing four core categories based on AI technology participation and interaction modes, breaking through the limitations of traditional film festival classifications based on duration and production modes.

🎭

生成式電影類

Generative Film Category

AI參與度：40%-70%
AI Participation: 40%-70%

主體敘事結構為線性，但視覺、聲音或敘事元素主要由AI系統生成。 評價重點在於人類創作者如何引導AI生成具有獨特美學風格的內容。

The main narrative structure is linear, but visual, audio, or narrative elements are primarily generated by AI systems. The evaluation focuses on how human creators guide AI to generate content with unique aesthetic styles.

參賽要求：Entry Requirements: 時長30分鐘以上（長片）、5-30分鐘（短片）Duration over 30 minutes (feature), 5-30 minutes (short)

技術特徵：

Technical Features:

  • • 保持傳統電影的線性敘事結構
  • • Maintains traditional film's linear narrative structure
  • • 大量使用AI生成的視覺、音頻或文本內容
  • • Extensive use of AI-generated visual, audio, or text content
  • • 人類創作者負責整體創意指導和後期整合
  • • Human creators responsible for overall creative direction and post-production integration
🌍

互動敘事與世界構建類

Interactive Narrative & World Building Category

AI參與度：50%-80%
AI Participation: 50%-80%

非線性的可探索體驗，觀眾能夠影響故事走向或自由探索AI輔助構建的虛擬世界。 強調用戶代理感和探索體驗。

Non-linear explorable experiences where audiences can influence story direction or freely explore AI-assisted virtual worlds. Emphasizes user agency and exploration experience.

參賽要求：Entry Requirements: 核心體驗時長不少於15分鐘，需提供可運行版本Core experience duration no less than 15 minutes, must provide runnable version

技術特徵：

Technical Features:

  • • 多分支敘事結構或開放世界設計
  • • Multi-branch narrative structure or open world design
  • • 實時響應用戶輸入的動態內容生成
  • • Dynamic content generation responding to user input in real-time
  • • 強調用戶代理感和探索體驗
  • • Emphasis on user agency and exploration experience
⚙️

程序化與涌現系統類

Procedural & Emergent Systems Category

AI參與度：70%-90%
AI Participation: 70%-90%

參賽作品本身是一個"系統"，能夠在每次運行時生成獨一無二、 不可預見的敘事或視覺體驗。評審重點是系統設計的精巧性。

The entry itself is a "system" capable of generating unique, unpredictable narrative or visual experiences with each run. The evaluation focuses on the sophistication of system design.

參賽要求：Entry Requirements: 展示至少10次不同運行結果，提供系統技術文檔Demonstrate at least 10 different run results, provide system technical documentation

技術特徵：

Technical Features:

  • • 高度的程序化生成邏輯
  • • High degree of procedural generation logic
  • • 具備涌現特性的複雜系統
  • • Complex systems with emergent characteristics
  • • 每次運行產生不同的結果
  • • Different results with each run

  • • AI驅動的行為邏輯和情感表達
  • • AI-driven behavior logic and emotional expression
  • • 具備一定程度的自主性和交互能力
  • • Possessing a certain degree of autonomy and interaction capability

範式革命：從邊緣到核心

Paradigm Revolution: From Margin to Core

HKAIIFF不僅是技術層面的升級，更是電影藝術本質的重新定義。 我們將AI從輔助工具提升為創作夥伴，將觀眾從被動觀看者轉變為主動參與者， 將藝術評價從主觀感受進化為量化評估，將商業模式從傳統贊助轉型為生態共贏。

HKAIIFF is not just an upgrade at the technical level, but a redefinition of the essence of cinematic art. We elevate AI from auxiliary tool to creative partner, transform audiences from passive viewers to active participants, evolve artistic evaluation from subjective feeling to quantitative assessment, and transition business models from traditional sponsorship to ecosystem win-win.
