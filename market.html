Platform Overview

Redefining film financing and distribution through blockchain and AI integration

Revolutionizing the Film Industry

The Hong Kong AI Film Market (AIF.Market) is a revolutionary blockchain platform designed to integrate artificial intelligence technology with traditional film production processes, creating a decentralized, transparent, and efficient new global film industry ecosystem.

The platform combines Hong Kong's unique advantage of East-West cultural fusion with cutting-edge technology to provide a creative value exchange network for global film creators, investors, and audiences.

Solving Industry Pain Points

High Investment, High Risk

Traditional film production has long cycles and high uncertainty in investment returns. Our tokenized approach distributes risk while providing liquidity.

Narrow Financing Channels

Small and medium-sized production companies struggle to secure adequate funding. Our platform democratizes access to global capital.

Opaque Value Distribution

Profit-sharing processes are complex and lack transparent records. Our smart contracts ensure fair and transparent revenue distribution.

Difficult Copyright Protection

Piracy issues are serious and rights protection costs are high. Our NFT-based copyright system provides immutable proof of ownership.

Global Distribution Barriers

Regional policy restrictions and cultural differences make cross-border distribution difficult. Our platform offers global reach with local adaptations.

AI Integration Challenges

Lack of standardized processes for AI in film production. Our platform provides frameworks for AI-human collaboration in creative processes.

Token Rights & Benefits

Revenue Sharing Rights

Token holders receive a proportional share of the film's revenue streams across all distribution channels, automatically distributed through smart contracts.

Creative Decision Making

Voting rights on key creative decisions during production, such as casting choices, marketing approaches, or distribution strategies.

Special Access & Perks

Exclusive access to premieres, behind-the-scenes content, director's cuts, and other special benefits based on token holding level.

Value Capture Mechanism

  • Service Fee Value Capture: Platform fees from transactions flow back to AIF token holders
  • Project Success Sharing: Ecosystem fund receives a small percentage of successful project revenue
  • AI Service Charges: Fees from AI tool usage within the platform ecosystem
  • Staking Economics: Token locking mechanisms that reduce circulating supply
