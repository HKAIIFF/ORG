Competition Overview

The HKAIIFF Exhibition and Competition Section is the world's most authoritative competition platform for AI-native film and media works, leading global trends and standards in AI film creation.

Global Platform

Attracting AI film creators from over 100 countries worldwide, establishing international standards for AI-native content.

Industry Recognition

Awards carry significant weight in the industry, opening doors to global distribution, financing, and collaboration opportunities.

Innovation Focus

Celebrating groundbreaking applications of AI technology in storytelling, production, and audience engagement.

What Makes Us Different

AI-Native Focus

Unlike traditional film festivals, we specifically celebrate works where AI technology plays a fundamental role in the creative process, from conception to final production.

  • Minimum 40% AI participation in creation process
  • Technical innovation in AI application
  • Human-AI collaboration storytelling

Comprehensive Evaluation

Our multi-dimensional evaluation system ensures fair assessment across technical innovation, artistic merit, commercial potential, and cultural impact.

  • Expert jury panels from multiple disciplines
  • Transparent scoring methodology
  • Audience participation in select categories

Evaluation Criteria

Our multi-dimensional evaluation system ensures comprehensive and fair assessment of all submissions across four key dimensions.

Technical Dimension

30%
  • Technical Innovation:

    Novel applications of AI technology in filmmaking

  • Technical Complexity:

    Sophistication of AI systems and integration

  • Execution Quality:

    Technical proficiency and production standards

Artistic Dimension

30%
  • Narrative Innovation:

    Creative storytelling approaches and structure

  • Audiovisual Aesthetics:

    Visual and sound design excellence

  • Emotional Expression:

    Ability to evoke emotion and connect with audiences

Commercial Dimension

20%
  • Market Potential:

    Commercial viability and market appeal

  • Audience Breadth:

    Potential reach across different demographics

  • Cost-Effectiveness:

    Efficient use of resources and budget

Cultural Dimension

20%
  • Cultural Value:

    Contribution to cultural discourse and heritage

  • Social Significance:

    Relevance to contemporary social issues

  • Cross-Cultural Nature:

    Ability to transcend cultural boundaries
