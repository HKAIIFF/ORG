About the Academy

The Hong Kong AI Film Academy (AIFA) is the world's first professional educational institution focused exclusively on the AI film field

Academy Background

The Hong Kong AI Film Academy (Hong Kong AI Film Academy Limited, AIFA) is jointly funded by the Hong Kong AI International Film Festival Association, the Hong Kong AI Film Market, and several internationally renowned technology companies and film companies.

Based in Hong Kong, an Asian international cultural and creative center, AIFA fully utilizes the unique advantage of connecting Eastern and Western artistic concepts, as well as Hong Kong's position as an important hub for finance, technology, and creative industries in the Asia-Pacific region.

Curriculum System

A comprehensive three-level education system providing a stepped cultivation path for students of all backgrounds

Three-Tier System

The academy adopts a three-level system, providing a stepped cultivation path according to students' starting points and goals. Our comprehensive curriculum includes:

  • 1

    Preparatory Certification Courses

    For beginners in the AI film field, focusing on quickly mastering hot skills and commercial monetization

  • 2

    Industry Talent Certification Courses

    Basic certification courses designed for all beginners in the AI film field, establishing overall cognition and basic skills

  • 3

    Professional Talent Certification Courses

    Providing in-depth cultivation and professional certification for specific career directions, covering 68 professional directions in seven major fields

Application & Admission

Join the next generation of AI film professionals

Admission Process

1

Online Application

Complete the online application form with your personal information, educational background, and program preferences. Submit any required documents including transcripts and recommendation letters.

2

Portfolio Submission

Submit examples of your work that demonstrate your creative and/or technical abilities. This can include film projects, visual art, coding samples, or other relevant work based on your program track.

3

Assessment & Interview

Complete a skills assessment relevant to your chosen program. Shortlisted candidates will be invited for an interview with faculty members to discuss your background, goals, and fit for the program.

4

Admission Decision

Receive your admission decision within 4-6 weeks of completing all application requirements. Upon acceptance, you'll receive information about enrollment, orientation, and next steps.
